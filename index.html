About GrayHound

GrayHound, an illustrious Biz-Tech quizzing contest, is a flagship event of Aparoksha - The Technical Fest of IIIT-Allahabad. This event will be conducted across various cities pan India to hunt for the best quizzer among the college students.

The quiz contest will unfold through a preliminary round to be held both online and offline. The top 30 teams will be selected and invited to the prestigious campus of IIIT Allahabad for the final round, that will be hosted under the aegis of acclaimed quiz masters.

One of the best quizzing event you will ever experience!

FAQ

Who can participate?

Unless you are an alien, you can participate in GrayHound.

Contestants can participate in teams consisting of two members. Although lonewolves(single member) are welcome too.

Yes, their is prize money in online round too.

Yes, there is a registration fee of 100 per team.
