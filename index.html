About GrayHound

GrayHound, is an all India Biz-Tech quiz contest. It is a flagship event of Aparoksha, IIIT-Allahabad. GrayHound Quiz aims to find the best quizzing talent among the college students.

The quiz contest consists of two rounds. A preliminary round to be held both online. The top 30 teams will be selected and invited to IIIT Allahabad for the grand finale

One of the best quizzing event you will ever experience!

FAQ

Who can participate?

Unless you are an alien, you can participate in GrayHound.

Do we participate as a team or lonewolves?

Contestants can participate in teams consisting of two members. Although lonewolves(single member) are welcome too.

What are the prizes?

There are prizes worth 30,000 to be won. There will be E-Certificates for all the participants. T-Shirts and goodies await the top teams too.

Is there a registration fee?

Yes, there is a registration fee of 100 per team.
