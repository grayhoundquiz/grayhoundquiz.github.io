    About The Event

    GrayHound, an illustrious Tech quizzing contest, is a flagship event of Aparoksha - The Technical cum Entrepreneurship Fest of IIIT-Allahabad. This event will be conducted across various cities pan India to hunt for the best quizzer among the college students.
    The quiz contest will unfold through a preliminary round to be held both online and offline. The top 30 teams will be selected and invited to the prestigious campus of IIIT Allahabad for the final round, that will be hosted under the aegis of acclaimed quiz masters.
